Corporate Graphics

At Illinois Mutual I was a member of the Marketing, Communications, and Research team. Working primarily on the marketing side, I designed and developed materials for our corporate website, social media, and print. One of my major area's of focus was in agent recruitment, where I created emails and landing pages designed to entice new agents to contract with Illinois Mutual.

Another major area of my work was print graphic design. Whether I was creating new pieces from scratch or updating existing materials, I had to work within the corporate brand guidelines while creating layouts that were approachable while conveying the naturally complicated minutiae of life insurance.
