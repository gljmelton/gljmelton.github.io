About

I'm a student at Bradley University studying interactive media with a concentration in game design. I'm trying to make my mark as a solo indie developer. I love learning and the challenges that come with making a game on my own. I focus on minimal and low poly design, aiming for unique visual styles, and unique narrative elements.

I draw a lot of my inspiration from science fiction as well as the works of H.P. Lovecraft, and enjoy blending elements from multiple sources to create unique works.
